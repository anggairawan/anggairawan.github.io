Research Interests

My research sits at the intersection of energy-efficient embedded AI, IoT sensor systems, and real-time control. I am interested in how machine learning models can be compressed and deployed directly on resource-constrained microcontrollers — enabling local inference without cloud dependency. My broader interest covers the hardware–software co-design of intelligent sensor nodes capable of autonomous data acquisition, onboard processing, and coordinated behaviour in large-scale distributed deployments.
